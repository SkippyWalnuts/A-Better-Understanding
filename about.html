A Better Understanding

"Ask with genuine curiosity: why do YOU think that is?"
a_becca_understanding

Connecting employees across increasingly digital work environments, whilst paving the way for equitably inclusive business leaders

Over 20 years of Personal Assistant (PA) experience left Founder & Consultant Advisor, Becca Early, feeling complicit with the lack of attendee diversity of C-Suite board-rooms. Eager to join the fight for Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Becca understood why many projects are unsustainable and first cut when budgets tighten: DEI needs Assistants.

A Better Understanding’s framework draws upon Becca’s Power of the PA: time efficiency, an appreciation for simplicity, and the confidence to speak comfortably with anyone, regardless of business titles. After all, who better to create sustainable, budget-friendly bridges between employees than the Assistants already doing so within the busiest of business schedules?

Get to know your business, without the survey-fatigue!

With a focus on simplistic scheduling, Becca led virtual Reverse Mentoring with international digital-marketing agency, dentsu, empowering Mentors to lead conversations with their perspectives on inclusion-related topics, and empowering Mentees to lean into vulnerabilities and become more inclusive business leaders.

Measuring success with attendance and participant reviews was the only PR necessary; 5-star ratings and consistent engagement meant participant numbers doubled by the second year, with many more applicants wait-listed across eager service lines.
